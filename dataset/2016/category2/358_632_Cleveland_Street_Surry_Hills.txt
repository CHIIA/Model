NSW Commercial sale, 358-632 Cleveland Street, Surry Hills A Chinese investor has paid $6.66m
for an inner Sydney building housing the popular Porteno Restaurant. The building, featuring a ground
and mezzanine level with an internal area of 750 sq m, is fully leased and was sold on a yield of 5.36
per cent. Porteno has been consistently placed on the Gourmet Traveller national top 50 restaurants
list. The lease runs until 2020 plus a further five-year option.

BUYER: Private investor VENDOR: Private investor SIZE: 750sq m internal PRICE: $6.66m AGENT:
Andy Hu and Linda Zhu, Knight Frank

VICTORIA Residential development site sale, 15-35 Thistlethwaite Street, South Melbourne A
Chinese developer has bought a residential development site in South Melbourne from Circa
Property for $8m. The property has an approval for an eight-level mixed use development of 83
apartments and commercial space. The deal was struck on a rate of $96,385 per unit.
