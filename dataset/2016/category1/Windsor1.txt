NSW Townhouse development site, 91 Windsor Road, Baulkham Hills Chinese developer Apex
Properties has netted $9.6 million selling a high-profile site with approval for freestanding townhouses
in Sydney’s northwest. The property was sold to a Parramatta developer, who will turn it into 26
freestanding Torrens title townhouses. The site is in a region that is gentrifying quickly. The
townhouses will range in internal size from about 168sq m to 191sq m with land areas of 280sq m to
320sq m.
