LP  Melbourne  site  fetches $12m

An offshore  Chinese  developer has paid $12.1  million  for a future  residential  development  site  in West
Melbourne. The  sale  of the  commercial property  which stands on a 1273-square-metre  site  at 33-43 Dudley
Street and is currently leased at a land rate of over $9500 a sq  m . It was  sold  without a planning permit by Josh
Rutman, Mark Wizel, Ed Wright and Julian White of CBRE. LARRY SCHLESINGER

TD  Mustera grabs East Perth landListed small-cap Perth investor and developer Mustera  Property  has paid $4
million  for a 2333-square-metre land parcel on the corner of Plain Street and Royal Street, in East Perth, 1.5
kilometres from the CBD. The  property  has a 50-bay car  park  leased to Wilson Parking and is earmarked for
future development. Mustera has also entered into a three-month option period to  purchase  an adjoining  property
at 45 Royal Street for $8.5  million . LARRY SCHLESINGER

Colliers buys US  firm

Colliers International has bought a controlling  stake  in US project management  group  Strategic Business
Solutions to expand its capabilities and add "best-in-class"  energy  sustainability management and commissioning
expertise. SBS, which is based in Connecticut, manages about $US1  billion  ($1.3  billion ) of projects. ROBERT
HARLEY

Family home for  sale

Adelaide's Shahin family, who had an estimated worth of $455  million  in last year's BRW Rich 200 List and who
controls the On The Run and Smokemart retail chains, has put on the market a six-bedroom Woodville home in the
street where they bought their first petrol station. LJ Hooker Kensington agent Nick Ploubidis said the family was
expecting a similar price to the $1.2  million  they got for a neighbouring  property  for last year. MICHAEL BLEBY
