NSW Site sale, 27 & 29 Gordon Street, Burwood A private Chinese investor has purchased a
development site in Sydney’s northwest for $5.8 million. The property is between the Burwood and
Strathfield train stations. Knight Frank senior director Dominic Ong said that Asian investors were
looking at sites in Sydney’s inner and middle ring suburbs.

PRICE: $5.8m BUYER: Private investor SELLER: Mr and Mrs Wu AREA: 933sq m AGENTS: Andy
Hu and Dominic Ong, Knight Frank

Office lease, 2 Park Street, Sydney Economos Chartered Accountants has leased an office suite in
the Citigroup Centre in the Sydney CBD at a net annual rental of $533,655. The term of the lease is
seven years. Knight Frank associate director Thomas Fredriksen said Economos had upgraded from
their previous office at 500 George Street.

RENT: $885 per square metre TENANT: Economos Chartered Accountants LESSOR: GPT Group
and Charter Hall Group AREA: 603 per sq m AGENT: Thomas Fredriksen, Knight Frank

VICTORIA Office lease, 173-175 Exhibition Street, Melbourne Chinese restaurant operator Hua
Chuan Group will open its first Melbourne outlet after agreeing to lease a CBD property for $155,000
per annum. The term of the lease is five years. Fitzroys agent Dan Noonan said Hua Chuan operated
three Chinese hotpot restaurants abroad. The Exhibition Street restaurant will serve modern Sichuan
food.
