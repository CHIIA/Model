LP  Broadwater ditched

Queensland's Labor government has stuck by its election promise to disallow a multibillion-dollar casino and cruise
ship terminal development on Wavebreak  Island  in the Gold Coast Broadwater. The ASF consortium, which wants
to build the complex, called on the government to allow the approval process to continue. MATTHEW CRANSTON

TD  Greensborough  buy

A private  Chinese  buyer has  purchased  a  site  at 273-275 Para Road in Melbourne's Greensborough for $3.1
million to develop a 75-unit  residential  complex. The project is set to be a landmark development in the area and is
close to the Greensborough train station. A private investor  sold  the 1615-square-metre  property  with a planning
permit. CBRE's Scott Orchard and David Minty, who inked the deal, said offshore  Chinese  investors are moving
away from city sites and into suburban ones. SU-LIN TAN

Lifestyle pipeline

ASX-listed retirement homes developer Lifestyle Communities has acquired a 225-home  site  in Australand's
Berwick Waters  estate  in Clyde North, Victoria for $11.5 million. Settlement and construction is set to occur in April
2016 with the contract conditional on a planning permit approval. SU-LIN TAN

Frasers' CBD play

Singapore-listed Frasers  Commercial  Trust has made a foray into the Melbourne office  property  market with a
deal to  buy  office building 357 Collins Street, from an Australand  property  trust for $222.5 million. Frasers,
controlled by Thai billionaire Charoen Sirivadhanabhakdi,  purchased  Australand last year for $2.6 billion. The
Singapore fund has bought the 25-storey 31,920-square-metre freehold  property  from Australand  Property
Holdings (Collins St No.1). SU-LIN TAN.

GWOF gets A-

Standard & Poor's has assigned an 'A-' issuer credit rating to GPT Wholesale Office Fund. S&P credit analyst Craig
Parker said the rating reflected GWOF's portfolio of very high-quality office  assets , an exposure to variable tenant
demand, and a conservative investment and operating strategy. ROBERT HARLEY
