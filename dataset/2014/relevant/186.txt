CFS Retail Property Trust Group has appointed Richard Jamieson as chief financial officer. Mr
Jamieson is the acting general manager, superannuation, marketing and direct, for BT Financial
Group. Mr Jamieson was formerly chief financial officer for Westpac New Zealand. Matthew Cranston

Listed developer Tamawood has appointed Timothy Bartholomaeus as managing director. He has
been with Tamawood since 1996. Matthew Cranston

GNG Investment Holdings Pty Ltd has bought a Sydney office freehold for $7 million. The property at
10-18 Oxford Square in Darlinghurst was offloaded by HMP Finance. The building has five separate
tenancies, three of which are vacant. The 8.5 per cent yield was based on an estimated fully leased
net income. It was purchased prior to auction by a Chinese investor who holds other assets in
Australia, including in the mining and winery sectors. Michael Khouri and Steam Leung of Colliers
International handled the deal. Mercedes Ruehl
A local developer has acquired a 1.925-hectare infill development site in Cairnlea in Melbourne's west
for $6.41 million at auction. The site at 93 Furlong Road is zoned for mixed use. It was marketed on
behalf of the Brimbank City Council by Fitzroys agents James Gregson and David Bourke. Larry
Schlesinger
