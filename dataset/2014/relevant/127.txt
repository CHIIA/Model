Property developer Payce has asked senior noteholders to amend the conditions of the notes so it
can raise more capital through subordinated unsecured notes. Robert Harley
DEXUS Property Group has appointed Paul Oates as group general manager, development. He
was head of development at Woolworths. He starts in December and moves to Melbourne next year.
Mercedes Ruehl

Listed WA developer Finbar will develop a $150 million apartment block on Adelaide Terrace, East
Perth, in a joint venture with Perth investment company Perth Upper China Hotel. The company
paid $15 million for the 3400-square-metre site. Finbar will finance and redevelop the site and share
the development profit of the 200-apartment project. Larry Schlesinger

Sydney builder Michael Iannello has been sentenced to 100 hours of community service for
managing Vibo Constructions despite declaring bankruptcy. Iannello pled guilty and was convicted in
Sydney's Downing Centre Local Court, the Australian Securities and Investments Commission said.
Michael Bleby
The NSW government's Glebe property, with potential for a 100-unit redevelopment, is for sale.
Savills' Stuart Cox is selling the 5556-square- metre property at 357 Glebe Point Road. It was once
a juvenile detention. It will be sold with a leaseback agreement to the government for two years. The
historic Bidura House, also on the site, will be kept. Mercedes Ruehl

State developer Places Victoria has booked a turnaround $43.5 million profit for the 2013-14 financial
year. Its annual loss had surged to $192 million in the 2013 fiscal year after deep write-downs in its
property portfolio and an ill-considered broadband rollout at its housing estates. Nick Lenaghan
