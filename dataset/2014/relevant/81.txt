Federation Centres has revalued its directly owned portfolio of shopping centres by 2.1 per cent,
adding $84.3 million to its worth at December, 2013. The 57-asset portfolio now stands at
$4.28 billion. Chief executive Steven Sewell said the increase came from net income growth and the
first material signs of cap rate compression. Nick Lenaghan

Growthpoint Properties Australia has struck new leasing deals in Brisbane covering 4000 square
metres, but still has 3500 square metres of Brisbane space to lease. Managing director Timothy
Collyer said the group's portfolio was 98 per cent full at December 31, with an average lease expiry
of 6.5 years. "We anticipate 2014 will be a better year for office market leasing activity than 2013," he
said. Larry Schlesinger

CBRE has promoted Alex Lowth to the newly created position of chief administrative and financial
officer in Asia Pacific. Based in Hong Kong, he will be responsible for shared services functions like
regional finance, human resources, information technology, business continuity and M&A. Robert
Harley
Singaporean property developer World Class Land, a subsidiary of the Aspial Corporation, has
gained FIRB approval to buy the Australia 108 site on Melbourne's Southbank for $42.3 million plus
GST. The vendor is a consortium that includes architect Nonda Katsalidis, investor Adrian Valmorbida
and developer Benni Aroni. CBRE handled the sale. Larry Schlesinger
