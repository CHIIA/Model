MARKET MANAGES TO CLOSE HIGHER IN RANGE TRADING ON LOW VOLUME

Our market, down for much of the day, managed a late rise of 7.6 points to 5518.9, touching a high of
5527.3 and a low of 5499.7 during the day. Volume remained low, worth $3.4 billion. Aussie 10 year
bond yield were down 4 points to 3.43%, the $A is at 93.36US c, up about 70 points from last evening.
The banks weakened slightly while resources rose on better than expected China GDP and IP numbers.

*In stock specifics, Fortescue rose 27c to $4.58 on 33.24m shares, partly on a strong quarterly and partly
on the iron ore price holding up, Atlas rose 1c to 60c on 14.5m shares.

*Sirius rose 51c to $3.95 on 5.9m shares on its new discoveries.

*Buxton Resources rose 7c to 43.5c on 1.3m shares in tandem with Sirius.

*Boart Longyear closed down 4.7c to 8.8c on 23.58m shares on its report today.

*Orocobre rose 15c to $2.78 on 1.1m shares on its lithium project.
