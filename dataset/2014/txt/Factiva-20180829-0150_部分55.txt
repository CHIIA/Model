China buys dairy: Chinese State-owned conglomerate Bright Food Group is set to buy West
Australian company Mundella, self-described as WA's "premier dairy company".

Bright will purchase Mundella through its Australian subsidiary, Manassen Foods, expanding the
Chinese conglomerate's overseas reach and boosting its exposure to the growing Asian dairy
market.

In 2011 Bright Food's paid $530 million for a 75 per cent of Manassen, whose brands includes
Sunbeam.
It also owns 51pc of New Zealand dairy producer Synlaid and is now in talks with Israeli
manufacturer Tnuva.
