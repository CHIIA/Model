Walsh’s $1b China deal WEST Australian meat processor V&V Walsh has signed a $1 billion deal to
supply boxed meat to China’s largest red meat importer. The Bunbury processor, who struck the deal
with China’s Grand Farm and the Inner Mongolian Government in June, will need an extra 500,000
lambs and 30,000 cattle a year in the first stage of the project.
