SYDNEY - National Australia Bank has moved to settle a multi-million dollar class action over unfair
fees. (NAB update to come)

SYDNEY - Department store Myer insists says gloomy shoppers are a challenge as it posts a flat first
quarter trade result. (MYER WRAP to come)

SYDNEY - Consumers haven't been this depressed since the global financial crisis. (CONSUMER
WESTPAC, wrap to come)

clients' money. (CBA wrap to come, pics available)

MELBOURNE - The Commonwealth Bank is cautiously optimistic about 2015, predicting slight rises in
consumer spending and demand for credit from businesses. (CBA Outlook to come)

worried a push to change current ownership laws could hurt the cause. (SEVEN WEST MEDIA, on file)

SYDNEY - A drop off in government advertising is set to deliver a hit to Seven West Media's first half
profit but chairman Kerry Stokes remains upbeat about the group's ability to lure in advertisers. (SEVEN
WEST, wrap to come)
PERTH - Iron ore miner Fortescue Metals Group is confident that Chinese growth will support the
company in the future. (FORTESCUE, wrap to come, pics available)

to continue to remain subdued in line with general economic conditions there (DULUX UPDATE sent)
