VICTORIA Office building sale, 12 Studley Street, Abbotsford An owner-occupier in the office fitout
business has paid $900,000 for a modern office building at Abbotsford in Melbourne’s inner-eastern
suburbs. The deal was brokered by Teska Carson. The three-storey, 245sq m property was sold by
its current owner-occupier, Kaiser Bass, with vacant possession.

SOUTH AUSTRALIA Industrial sale, 10-12 CarSten Road, Gepps Cross B & MJ Steel has
purchased an industrial property in Adelaide’s north for $850,000. JLL agent David Ludlow said the
high-clearance office and warehouse suited the purchaser, which delt with oversized lengths of
steel.
