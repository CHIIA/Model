Dragon Mountain Gold recently released their Annual Report. The updated profile of Robert Gardner,
CEO and Executive Chairman is shown below.

Mr Robert Gardner &#61492; Chairman (Executive) Experience and Qualifications &#61492; Mr Gardner
is a Perth based business proprietor, with over 27 years' experience in the mining industry. Mr Gardner
has developed a number of projects that are now major assets of ASX listed companies and has
extensive experience in the China region. He was the original founder and funder of the Lixian Project,
prior to its acquisition by the Company. Mr Gardner is also a major shareholder in the Company.
Interest in Shares and Options &#61492; 54,316,817 ordinary Shares in Dragon Mountain Gold Limited.
Directorships held in other listed entities &#61492; Executive Chairman of Nickelore Limited, from
October 2010 to present.
