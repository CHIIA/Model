NSW Pub sale, 484 Princes Highway, Rockdale A private investor has purchased the Grand Hotel
Rockdale is Sydney’s south for about $25 million. The hotel was sold by the Goff family after 35
years of ownership. It has a 24-hour liquor licence and 30 gaming machines. JLL’s Sam Handy said
the pub was keenly contested.

PRICE: $25m BUYER: Private investor SELLER: Goff family AGENT: Sam Handy, JLL Hotels &
Hospitality

Site sale, 344-350 Canterbury Road, Canterbury A private Chinese developer has purchased four
houses in Sydney’s southeast for a total of $8m. The properties were acquired from separate
homeowners. The zoning allows a mixed-use development.

PRICE: $8m BUYER: Private developer SELLER: Private homeowners AGENTS: Ari Danas and
Michael Ajaka, JLL

Industrial lease, 28 Barcoo Street, Chatswood Linfield Pacific has leased industrial space on
Sydney’s North Shore at a gross annual rental of $71,750. The high-clearance warehouse,
mezzanine office with car parking has been leased for three years.
