Minco Silver Corp. said Aug. 15 that it acquired a 5% stake in Gold Road Resources Ltd. for investment
purposes.
Gold Road's principal asset is the Gruyere gold project in Western Australia. The project has a
JORC-compliant measured and indicated resource of 40.19 million tonnes at 1.22 g/t of gold and
inferred resource of 56.74 million tonnes at 1.24 g/t of gold.

Minco Silver added that it will continue to look for other opportunities in silver- and gold-dominant projects
outside China.
The company is developing its Fuwan silver project in China's Guangdong province.
