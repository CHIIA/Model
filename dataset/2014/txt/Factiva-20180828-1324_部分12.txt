AUSTRALIAN DAILY STOCK REPORT
Icon Energy Limited (ASX:ICN), Australia's 48th largest energy company by market cap, decreased
0.20c (or 1.5%) to close at 13.0c. Compared with the All Ordinaries Index which rose 7.1 points (or 0.1%)
in the day, this represented a relative price change of -1.6%. The volume was 0.2 times the average
trading of 497,979 shares. Investors are eyeing Icon Energy earnings which will be released July 29.
