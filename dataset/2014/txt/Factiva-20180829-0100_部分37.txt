The board of directors of Stonewall Resources Limited ("Company") wishes to announce the
appointment of Dr.James Jian Liu as a director of the Company effective from 9 May 2014.Dr. Liu has
been a Director of Stonewall Mining Pty Ltd since 2011 and is well versed in the company.Dr. Liu is a
Managing Partner of Hanhong Private Equity Investment Company and a founding member of Smart
Vision Investment Group Limited, which is a significant shareholder of the Company.Dr. Liu has over 20
years entrepreneurial experience in China, the United States of America (USA) and Africa.

James Jian Liu, Director
Dr. Liu has been a Director of Stonewall Mining Pty Ltd since 2011 and is well versed in the company.
Dr. Liu is a Managing Partner of Hanhong Private Equity Investment Company and a founding member
of Smart Vision Investment Group Limited, which is a significant shareholder of the Company.
Dr. Liu has over 20 years entrepreneurial experience in China, the United States of America (USA) and
Africa. He has held several Directorships including AENY, a USA coal mining company, and Stonewall
Mining Pty Ltd. He was the co founder and managed two high-tech companies in the Silicon Valley and
was a former Banker with the Citi Group.
