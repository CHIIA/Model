John Haggman

John Haggman is a geologist with over 22 years of broad international experience and holds a Bachelor
of Science (geology) from Macquarie University, Sydney and is a Member of the Australian Institute of
Geoscientists.
He has been involved in all aspects of exploration from the assessment, discovery acquisition and
feasibility of projects and has worked throughout South East Asia, Australia, New Zealand, South and
Central America, India, China and USA.
Previous positions includes senior geologist for Cyprus Gold Australia, Vice President-Exploration for
Arimco N.L and Vice President Exploration for Climax Arimco Mining Corporation. He is currently is a
director of Titan Mines Limited, NQ Metals Pty Limited and Perlco Pty Limited and is an independent
consultant to the mining industry.

John Haggman has been a Director since June 2008.
