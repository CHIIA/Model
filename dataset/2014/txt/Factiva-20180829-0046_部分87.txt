FRANCE SELLS GDF SUEZSTAKE FOR $2 BILLION
The French government said it sold a 3.1% stake in energy groupGDF Suez for about 1.5 billion
euros or $2.04 billion, which will help finance the acquisition of a stake in Alstom.

TREASURY WINE DOUSES EARNINGS FEARS
KKR & Co. is under pressure to raise its 3.05 billion Australian dollar or $2.86 billion bid for Treasury
Wine Estates after the winemaker cooled fears of a profit warning.
ETIHAD TO BUY 49% OF ITALIAN AIRLINE ALITALIA
Etihad Airways confirmed it will buy 49% of Italy's troubled Alitalia in what is likely to be the Abu Dhabi
airline's biggest investment in a foreign carrier to date.

ALLERGAN SHAREHOLDERS SUPPORT VOTE ON TAKEOVER OFFER
Valeant Pharmaceuticals International CEO Michael Pearson said the company is nearing its goal of
rounding up enough support from Allergan Inc. shareholders to secure a vote on its $53 billion hostile
bid for the Botox maker.

FDA ORDERS GSK TO REVIEW ITS GLOBAL MANUFACTURING

The U.S. Food and Drug Administration has ordered GlaxoSmithKline to review its manufacturing
operations globally after finding that its Canadian subsidiary violated quality requirements during the
manufacture of its flu vaccine FluLaval, U.K. newspaper The Guardian reported on its website.

GENERAL MOTORS PLANS TO OFFER A BROAD PAYOUT

A fund set up by General Motors to compensate victims of crashes linked to defective ignition switches
could offer payments for anyone injured or killed in a crash in which the car's air bags didn't deploy,
people familiar with the situation said.
BEST BUY CONSIDERS OPTIONS FOR ITS CHINA BUSINESS
Best Buy is said to be considering options, including a sale or partnership, for its Chinese business
and is working with Bank of America Merrill Lynch on its options.
LEND LEASE SELLS U.K. MALL FOR $1.18 BILLION
Australian developer Lend Lease Groupsold the Bluewater shopping mall on the outskirts of London to
Land Securities for US$1.18 billion.
RELIANCE COMMUNICATIONS RAISES $995 MILLION

Reliance Communications Ltd. is selling shares to institutional investors and founders to raise about
$995 million as it aims to pay off some of the high-interest loans which have been hurting its
profitability.

Australia's earnings from resources and energy exports will rise at a markedly slower rate next fiscal
year as weaker commodity prices offset a sharp increase in shipments to Asian buyers.

NATIONAL AUSTRALIA BANK SEES SOLID MORTGAGE DEMAND

Demand for mortgages in Australia should remain strong even as home-price growth moderates, the
head of personal banking at National Australia Bank said.
SINGAPORE EXCHANGE TO LAUNCH NEW GOLD CONTRACT
The Singapore Exchange will launch a new physically-delivered gold contract this year, the country's
trade and industry minister, Lim Hng Kiang said.

GOOGLE TO UNVEIL NEW TV SET-TOP BOX

Google Inc. is to unveil a new television set-top box on Wednesday as it races Amazon.com, Apple and
others to control digital content in the home.
