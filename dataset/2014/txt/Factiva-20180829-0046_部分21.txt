Shaun Bonett is a former commercial lawyer with a long-held interest in property. He bought his first
house, aged 18, in the Adelaide CBD-fringe suburb of Unley. By 24, he established his own property
investment company, the Precision Group. Initially the company purchased, developed and sold retail
properties but it is now involved in large-scale retail and commercial property developments. Bonett has
been backed by the Bank of China since 2011 after striking a new $250 million refinancing deal. Bonett
has stakes in and is a director of newly listed insurance comparison site iSelect and Litigation Lending
Services. LLS provides litigation funding in Australia and New Zealand.
