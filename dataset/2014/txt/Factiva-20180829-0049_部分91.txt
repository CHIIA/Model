The cupcake queen from My Kitchen Rules, Jenna Marie shows us her favourite things

I LIVE ...

On a 2.5 acre property with my husband Tyson and our chickens. We've been here eight months.

I LOVE MY HOME BECAUSE ...

We built it with so much love. My kitchen is the heart of the home as is my herb garden.

BUT I STILL NEED TO ...

Work on my English-style rose garden. And also my big vegetable garden, which is surrounded by
roses dedicated to my best friend who recently passed away.

DECORATING STYLE

Very French.

AS A CHILD I WANTED TO BE ...

A family law lawyer.

CAREER MOVE
I'm about to open a cake shop called Le Pink Petite Patisserie at 71 Payneham Rd, College Park.

BEST FRIEND
This is a framed picture of me and my best friend who I know watches over me while I'm cooking.

MY KITCHEN

Is the heart of our home. My mortar and pestle, above right, was a gift from my husband for
Christmas.

ON WEEKENDS, I LIKE TO ...
Read cookbooks, play in the garden, make bread in the wood oven, visit farmers markets and
cook for my family.

PINK KITCHENAID MIXER

It's one of my very favourite things. I use it for making delicious cakes and pastries!

I COULDN'T LIVE WITHOUT ...

My husband Tyson, my sister Billie, my brother Jake and my father-in-law Joe.

I COLLECT
China cups, teapots, cookware and cookbooks.

IF I COULD AFFORD IT, I WOULD LIVE IN

The country at Strathalbyn and build a strawberry farm.

COOK BOOKS

I love my cook books. I have bought them online, in Paris, Sydney and Melbourne.

ON MY WISHLIST IS ...

A new set of Global knives and a beautiful skincare set.

The world to me, it's right where my heart sleeps.
