NEWS BITES - PEOPLE IN BUSINESS

Pluton Resources Ltd (ASX:PLV) (Pluton or the Company) wishes to advise the following changes
following a restructure of its Board of Directors and Management.The Company wishes to announce the
resignation of Richard Mehan as Chairman and director and Tony Schoer and Russell Williams as
non-executive directors and the appointment Dr.Paul D"Sylva as a director and interim Chairman.Mr.Brett
Clark, Managing Director said "We thank the outgoing directors for their service and are fortunate, that
Dr.D"Sylva has joined the Board with considerable capital markets experience and, having worked with
him previously on funding initiatives for the Company, have first hand experience with his
capabilities.Dr.D"Sylva is a founding director of Empire Equity Limited, where he has led and arranged
over A$500M in funding transactions for a diverse range of resource and energy companies in equity,
debt and structured financings on a proprietary basis as well as from a network of institutional funding
partners.Mr.Michael Wyer has been appointed as Interim CFO of the Company.

Russell Williams
Mr. Williams spent 30 years with Alcoa Inc., the world's largest integrated alumina and aluminium
producer. Trained as a mechanical engineer at the University of Manchester, he has worked in a range of
roles from power generation to engineering, maintenance and human resources.
During his time at Alcoa he managed the company's West Australian bauxite mining operations and
later had oversight of all of Alcoa's global mining activities in Brazil, Jamaica and Suriname. In his final
years with Alcoa, Mr. Williams was based in Pittsburgh and was responsible for all activity at the Alcoa JV
in Guinea, Company Bauxite de Guinee (CBG), the third largest export bauxite producer in the world. On
his return to Australia he spent the majority of his time working on Alcoa projects in China and other
Asian countries.
