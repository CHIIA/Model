HONG KONG, December 4, SinoCast -- Oriental Unicorn Agricultural Group Limited (SEHK: 8120)
declared agreement inked by wholly owned Rosy Path with Zhao Hui, agreeing to subscribe for a 55%
stake in the latter for AUD 2.2257 million, thus indirectly holding Viplus, an operator of a dairy workshop
with history of longer than 120 years in Victoria, Australia, engaged in formula milk powder processing
and production.
Viplus planned to expand in China, forming local professional sales teams to expand nationwide infant
milk powder distribution network, being one of the few Australian companies to get approval from the
Certification and Accreditation Administration for processing infant formula milk powder.
