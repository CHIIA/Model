LONDON--Leyshon Energy Ltd. (LEN.LN) said Monday it plans to expand the scope of the testing
program on its Zijinshan Gas Project and said it is in talks over buying oil and gas assets in China.

MAIN FACTS:

-Will increase the number of flow tests, testing 3+ zones in Well ZJS7, not one as previously planned

-Depending on results, plans to drill and flow-test a further well, ZJS8, in the northern part of the
license area
-Program to start shortly; will be completed by end-October at cost of up to $4 million excluding
overhead and supervision costs
-Technical team continues to be strengthened with the appointments of Frank Fan as Operations
manager and Lyn Liu as Reservoir Engineer
-Acquisition of oil and gas assets in China: discussions are in hand but won't necessarily lead to a
transaction

-Shares at 0723 GMT up 7.7%.
