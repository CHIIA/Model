Sales
CBD A Chinese legal firm based in Sydney bought level six, 256 Queen Street, for $1.45 million. The
288 sq m unrefurbished strata office sold for more than $5000 per sqm, a record for the building. The
deal was brokered by CBRE's Tom Tuxworth, Chris Pharr and Ed Wright for a local investor.

HAWTHORN A local private investor has bought a 806-sq-m property at 551 Glenferrie Road for
$4.15 million. The building is occupied by tenant Klemms Business Brokers on a long-term lease worth
$260,000 a year. Brokering agent Andrew Morley of Morley Commercial said the sale represented a
yield of 6.2 per cent.
KOOYONG 479 Glenferrie Road sold for $1.3 million to private investors. Morley Commercial's
Andrew Morley said the yield was 4.2 per cent. Housing Koots restaurant, the 203 sq m site has an
income of $55,000 a year.

Leases

CBD IPA Personal, a recruitment agency linked to Employment Services Holdings, has agreed to a
$487,620 a year lease on level four, 271 Collins Street. Savills brokered the five-year lease for the
1134 sqm office owned by Fitzroys and Colliers.

FAIRFIELD Sokol Designer Furniture, a wholesaler specialising in designer furniture and quality
replicas, has leased an office, showroom and warehouse at 59 Steane Street for $107,000 a year. The
1682 sq m site includes 14 car spaces. ICR property group's Guy Naselli and Raff De Luise brokered
the lease.
