LOUDONG GN RES (00988.HK) enters into MOU on acquiring Sydney commercial property

LOUDONG GN RES (00988.HK) entered into the GNLC MOU with Mega Smart whereby the Group
has in principle agreed to sell the GNLC Sale Shares, which represent the entire issued share capital of
GNLC. GNLC holds 47.24% of the registered capital of Shanxi Loudong, which is principally engaged in
coal processing and production of coke and coal relevant chemicals in China and it is one of the major
operating subsidiaries of the Group.

LOUDONG GN RES also entered into a MOU in relation to the acquisition of a commercial property
in North Sydney, Australia.
