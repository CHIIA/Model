Louis van Aardt, a partner in Kott Gunning's Corporate, Commercial and Finance team, has been
invited to the join the board of the International Institute of Business and Technology Australia (IIBT) as
a non-executive director.
IIBT was established over 10 years ago in China and is now operating in Perth, Western Australia. IIBT
is a Government approved Higher Education and VET provider of pre-university, first year university and
English programs.

Offering fully accredited and nationally recognised diplomas and certificates, including the Diploma of
Business Administration general English and Certificates III & IV in Academic English, their programs
are designed to equip students with the essential skills for university and guarantee entry into IIBT's
partner Universities.
There are currently more than 90 students from all over the world including countries such as China,
India and Korea studying at IIBT's new purpose built high tech facility in Victoria Park.

More about Louis van Aardt
Louis recently acted for China Merchants Bank Ltd (Hong Kong Branch) in relation to a US$300m fixed
term loan facility to Karara Mining Limited (May 2014). This transaction was reported in the December
2014 issue of the Australasian Lawyer magazine.

He has held a number of directorships and is currently also on the boards of the Western Australian
Swimming Association Inc (Swimming WA) and The Global Good Foundation. Louis is a Graduate of
the Australian Institute of Company Directors (GAICD).
