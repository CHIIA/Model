IRON ORE, OIL STOCKS DOWN IN LOW VOLUME TRADE/$A SPIKES UP

ore miners while gold stocks were winners, but with subdued gains today following sharp rises
yesterday.
China's services PMI number for December, following the manufacturing PMI yesterday was also below
the previous month's, spooking investors. Banks, ex dividend also weakened as did oil stocks on the fall
in the price last night.
