AUSTRALIAN DAILY STOCK REPORT
Uranex Limited (ASX:UNX), Australia's 60th largest energy company by market cap, closed unchanged
at 22.0c. Compared with the All Ordinaries Index which fell 5.0 points (or 0.1%) in the day, this
represented a relative price increase of 0.1%. This has been exacerbated by high volume of 3.2 times
average. Investors are eyeing Uranex earnings which will be released July 28.
