Industrial sale, 179 Montague Road, South Brisbane

Infrastructure company Otswold Bros has bought a warehouse and office in South Brisbane for
$6.9 million.

PRICE: $6.9m

VICTORIA

Retail sales, 114 Russell Street, Melbourne
Private Chinese investors have acquired three retail properties on the ground floor of the Hero
complex in the Melbourne CBD for a total of $4.76m.
One investor acquired a property leased by patisserie Brioche by Philip for $1.05m and a store
occupied by fashion retailer Chiodo for $2.1m. A separate buyer bought the adjoining property for
$1.61m.

PRICE: $4.76m (total)

BUYER: Private investors

SELLER: Phillip Maynes and Peter Bartholomew

AREA: 536sq m

AGENT: Josh Rutman, Ed Wright and Mark Wizel, CBRE

gross annual rental of $944,000.

The term of the lease is three years.
