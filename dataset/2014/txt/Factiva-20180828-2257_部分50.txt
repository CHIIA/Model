Australia raised $4.9 billion by selling health insurer Medibank Private, marking the largest IPO of a
government-owned asset in the country since 1997.

---

China's central bank and the ECB moved to pump up global growth, sending stock markets soaring
but raising questions about the limitations of a seven-year effort to use monetary policy to address
economic problems.

---
Investors in the U.S. have poured cash into IPOs of energy-focused master limited partnerships,
known for their stable, high distributions. Now, some money managers are urging caution.

---

Hong Kong that could raise as much as $3.16 billion.

---

---
Pension-fund managers from the Netherlands to Canada lambasted private-equity executives at a

---

laws, as the country steps up scrutiny of casinos' efforts to prevent such activity.

---

complicate efforts to aid the nation's economy.
