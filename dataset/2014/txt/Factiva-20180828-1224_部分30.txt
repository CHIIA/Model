VICTORIA Industrial sale, 215 Browns Road, Noble Park Property fund manager Aspen Group has
sold an industrial property in Melbourne’s southeast for $20.8 million. On 5.8ha, it includes office and
warehouse buildings totalling 43,949sq m. Aspen Group chief executive Clem Salwin said: “The sale
leaves our industrial property at Spearwood as the last of the major non-core assets, where our
immediate asset management focus remains on leasing.” PRICE: $20.1m BUYER: Undisclosed
SELLER: Aspen Group AREA: 5.81ha (land), 43,949sq m (building) AGENT: Knight Frank
Mixed use sale, 627 High Street, Kew A Chinese investor has purchased a three-level commercial
building at an auction in Melbourne’s east for $2.69m. The building, in Kew’s Harp Village precinct, is
occupied by Hooking Bar & Restaurant and i2C Architects. CBRE agent Rorey James said there was
increased interest in Melbourne’s secondary retail market.

PRICE: $2.69m YIELD: 5.53 per cent BUYER: Chinese investor AGENTS: Rorey James, Justin
Dowers, Mark Wizel, CBRE
QUEENSLAND Retail sale, 30-50 Warrago Highway, Chinchilla A private Melbourne syndicate has
purchased the The Village Travel Centre at Chinchilla in Queensland for $10.1m. The two-complex
property spans a 10,564sq m site. One lot covers 3942sq m of land with the 838sq m of building
space leased to Fast Food Village. The second lot is 6622sq m, Caltex and KFC leasing the building.

PRICE: $10.1m BUYER: Melbourne-based syndicate AREA: 10,564sq m (land)AGENT: Stephen Kidd,
Colliers International
