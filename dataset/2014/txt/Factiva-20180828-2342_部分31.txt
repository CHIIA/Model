Graeme Booth, CFO and MD
Graeme is a Chartered Accountant with a broad range of finance, commercial and coal experience over
20 years.
Most recently Graeme was Group CFO of Ambre Energy Limited (operator and joint owner of two
producing coal mines and developer of two coal export terminals in the USA).

He has also held senior positions with Royal Bank of Scotland (RBS), ABN Amro, and Deloitte. In these
roles Graeme gained international experience in capital markets (equity and debt), financial risk
management, accounting and audit in Australia, Hong Kong, London and South Africa.

As a Managing Director at RBS/ABN Amro, Graeme held senior positions across origination, trading and
risk management including, inter alia, Head of Strategic Equity Solutions and Co- Head of Equity and
Equity Derivatives Trading in Asia. Graeme was also a key member of the Global Equities Origination
and Asian Equities management teams.
