Mining magnate Gina Rinehart is weeks away from completing the purchase of in Western Australia's
Kimberley region in the state's far north.

There are several conditions yet to be met before the deal is signed off, and ABC Rural understands the
main sticking point is a request to increase the allowable number of cattle to be run on the properties.

The request to increase stocking densities is being assessed by the WA Pastoral Board.

The stations are Moola Bulla, Mt Amhurst, Beefwood Park and Shamrock Station.

in the state's south-west.

Milne AgriGroup is owned by the Laitt family, and when the joint-venture was struck Graham Laitt said it
was expected that cattle production on those two stations would double.

"It's targeting growing beef production in the north of WA and positioning ourselves, and the state, for the
