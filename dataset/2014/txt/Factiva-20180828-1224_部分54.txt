AUSTRALIAN DAILY STOCK REPORT
Icon Energy Limited (ASX:ICN), Australia's 39th largest energy company by market cap, traded
between an intraday low of 16.0c and a high of 16.70c. The price range has expanded in the last two days
(from 0.20c two days ago to 0.70c today) which, accompanied by a price rise, is a bullish signal. The
average daily volatility of 3.0% places the stock in the 3rd quartile in the market meaning it is mildly
volatile. Since the start of trading its volatility (highest price minus lowest price/lowest price) of 4.4% was
1.5 times the average daily volatility of 3.0%, up from 1.2% on Thursday and 1.2% on Wednesday. A
price rise on high volatility is a bullish signal. The stock price increased 0.20c (or 1.2%) to close at 16.70c.
Compared with the All Ordinaries Index which rose 3.3 points (or 0.06%) in the day, this represented a
relative price increase of 1.2%.
