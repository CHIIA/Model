Alistair Currie was appointed COO of Australia and New Zealand Banking Group (ASX:ANZ) three
years ago on October 15, 2011. The present value of AUD1,000 (PV1000) invested on the appointment
date is $2,018, for a capital gain of $686 and dividend reinvested of $332. In the same period the shares
were up 68.6% from $18.6 to $31.4.

MANAGEMENT SCORECARD FOR AUSTRALIA AND NEW ZEALAND BANKING GROUP LIMITED:

Creation of shareholder value:

- Market Capitalization has increased by $31.4 billion from $55.2 billion to $86.6 billion since
appointment. This increase comprises cumulative retained earnings of $1.9 billion and Created Market
Value of $29.5 billion. The Created Market Value multiple, defined by the change in MCap for every
AUD1 of retained earnings is exemplary at 16.2.

Biography

Alistair Currie, COO
Alistair Currie is Group Chief Operating Officer, responsible for technology, shared services and
operations including ANZ's hubs, property and major projects.
Prior to his appointment, Alistair was Managing Director, Transaction Banking, Institutional.

Page104of282©2018Factiva,Inc.Allrightsreserved.Alistair joined ANZ from HSBC in November in 2008, where he had 18 years of global experience in
transaction services, trade finance and commercial banking. He was previously President and CEO of
HSBC Taiwan, where he led a staff of more than 3,500. Other positions include senior roles in Asia, the
Middle East, the UK and the US, including COO of Wells Fargo HSBC Trade Bank in San Francisco.

As Regional Head of Trade Services for HSBC Asia Pacific, Alistair was responsible for trade finance,
supply chain and structured trade in 21 countries, and further developed HSBC's market-leading trade
finance position in the region.
Alistair was born in Scotland and grew up in Hong Kong. He holds a Master of Science (Management)
from Imperial College, University of London, UK and a Bachelor of Science (Design & Technology) from
Brunel University, UK.
