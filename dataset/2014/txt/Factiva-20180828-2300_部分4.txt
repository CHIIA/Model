FOOD CHINA has moved quickly to cultivate new sources of milk in Australia in the wake of the
free-trade deal between the countries.
Australian companyFreedom Foods Group has signed a memorandum of understanding with
China’s New Hope Group to develop a long-term supply of milk with the establishment of new
large-scale farms in southeast Australia.

The farms will be managed by the Perich Group, which holds a stake of about 60 per cent in
Freedom Foods.
New Hope will be a core investor and partner, while continuing to market and distribute dairy
products into China through New Hope Dairy.New Hope, one of the largest suppliers of meat, eggs
and dairy products in China, has established an investment fund of up to $500 million to be used in
the development of agricultural and food processing in Australia.
