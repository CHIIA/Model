Rockwood Holdings Inc. said May 28 that it has completed the acquisition of a 49% ownership interest in
Talison Lithium Pty. Ltd. to create a joint venture with Chinese lithium companySichuan Tianqi Lithium
Industries Inc.
The company funded the US$475 million purchase with cash on hand, it said.

Lithium producer Talison mines and processes the lithium-bearing mineral spodumene at its
Greenbushes Lithium mine in Western Australia.
Talison produces two types of lithium concentrates, technical-grade, low-iron lithium concentrates used in
manufacturing and a high-yielding chemical-grade lithium concentrates used to produce lithium chemicals
which are then used to manufacture lithium-ion batteries.

Rockwood noted that the Greenbushes operation currently has an estimated 40-year mine life.
