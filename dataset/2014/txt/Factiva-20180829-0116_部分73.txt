LONDON--Bellzone Mining PLC (BZM.LN), which is developing iron ore and Ni/Cu assets in Guinea,
West Africa, said Friday it is currently in discussions with one of its major shareholders, China
Sonangol International Singapore Pte Ltd., over securing additional equity capital which may or may
not lead to an offer being made for the company.

MAIN FACTS:

-Bellzone, which was responding to the movement in its share price, cautioned that At this stage there
can be no certainty that the preliminary discussions will result in additional equity capital being secured,
nor as to the terms on which any proposal might be made or pursued.
-Shares at 1015 GMT up 1 pence, or 32%, at 4 pence valuing the company at 29 million pounds.
