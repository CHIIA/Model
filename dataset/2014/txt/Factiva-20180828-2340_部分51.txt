QUEENSLAND Hotel sale, Kangaroo Point The Il Mondo Boutique Hotel in inner Brisbane has been
purchased by a Chinese investment syndicate for $13.5 million. The hotel occupies a 1580sq m site.
There is a possibility of residential redevelopment on the parcel.

NSW Cinema sale, 447-453 King Georges Road, Beverly Hills A local private investor has purchased
the Beverly Hills Cinemas and Subway Restaurant at Beverly Hills in Sydney’s southwest for about
$6.5m. The site is about 1450sq m and features six cinemas. JLL director of sales and investments
Michael Ajaka said that it was the first Australian cinema to be sold in the past year. “We received
inquiries from local investors as well as offshore. The strong interest highlights the value of cinemas as
part of an entertainment asset class — particularly in a great location such as this,” Mr Ajaka said.
