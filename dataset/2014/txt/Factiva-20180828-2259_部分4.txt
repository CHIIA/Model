Fosun declared that its subsidiary Transcendent Resources has purchased Roc Oil for US$400 million.
With this acquisition, now Transcendent will be able to access Roc's oil assets in Australia, Malaysia
and the North Sea.

Australia-based Roc Oil is an oil and gas company. It has annual revenues of US$250 million.APS
