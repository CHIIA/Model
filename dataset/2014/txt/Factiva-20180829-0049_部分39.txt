AQUILA REJECTS BID FROM MINERAL RESOURCES
A Chinese-led group regained the upper hand in bidding for Aquila Resources Ltd. after the Australian
iron-ore miner rejected a rival proposal from Mineral Resources Ltd. valuing it at 1.54 billion Australian
dollars . (published 0046 GMT)

CHINA CENTRAL BANK CONFIRMS CHINA CONSTRUCTION BANK AS LONDON YUAN-CLEARING
BANK
China's central bank said Wednesday that it has chosen China Construction Bank Corp. as the first
yuan-clearing bank in London. (published 0157 GMT)

NEWCREST AGREES TO PENALTY FOR DISCLOSURE BREACHES
Newcrest Mining Ltd. said it had reached a settlement with Australia's corporate regulator to pay a 1.2
million Australian dollar penalty for breaching rules about disclosing market-sensitive information.
(published 0048 GMT)

BUSINESSMAN LEW LIFTS
STAKE IN TAKEOVER TARGET DAVID JONES
Australian businessman Solomon Lew has lifted his stake in David Jones Ltd. to just shy of 10%,
deepening uncertainty over an agreed takeover of the department store chain by South Africa's
Woolworths Holdings Ltd. . (published 0010 GMT)
