April 17 (Reuters) - Zijin Mining Group Co Ltd

* Says its Australian unit Norton Gold Fields Ltd to acquire Bullabulling Gold Ltd
* Says Norton Gold Fields offers to acquire all the issued shares in Bullabulling Gold at A$0.07
($0.07) per share, representing a 30 percent premium over Bullabulling's closing price on April 17
* Says the acquisition valued Bullabulling Gold at A$25.20 million
