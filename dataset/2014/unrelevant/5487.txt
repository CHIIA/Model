Martin Jacobsen, CEO, COO and MD
Martin Jacobsen joined WPG from his previous position as Vice President, Operations, with Golden
China Resources Limited, a gold mining and exploration company with project assets in China. Prior
to that he was Technical Director with Emperor Mines Limited and had earlier held senior management
positions in gold, chrome and platinum mining operations in South Africa. He has been project
manager for a number of projects in a wide range of commodities and mine types. Martin manages new
project acquisition and development, and was appointed the Company's CEO in April 2012. He joined
the Board on 16 October 2013.
