Queensland Commercial site sale, 1 Energy Circuit, Robina The mixed-use M1X Energy Centre in the
CBD Robina M1 Business Precinct development has sold to a private Chinese investor for about $4
million. The facility includes several business spaces between 198sq m and 628sq m, and is occupied
by the NeuroPhysics Training gym, beauty products manufacturer The Natalie Group, and Systems
Cabling Australia. The buyer will occupy the remaining two spaces.
PRICE: About $4m BUYER: Private Chinese investor SELLER: Private investor AREA: 3249sq m
(land), 1786sq m (building) AGENT: Darrell Irwin, Colliers International

Residential development site sale, Aruma Drive, Oakey Developer Richard Adams Homes has
purchased a 37-lot residential subdivision and a mixed development holding in the Darling Downs
region for $1.3m. The developer intends to begin construction immediately with house and land
packages from $350,000. Agent Mark Wynhoven of Ray White Commercial said the emergence of the
Surat Basin and infrastructure work in the area would result in new development. “This resource- rich
western region has been forecast to benefit from the thriving agriculture, manufacturing and energy
industries,” he said.

PRICE: $1.3m BUYER: Richard Adams Homes SELLER: Private investor AREA: 433,700sq m
AGENT: Mark Wynhoven, Ray White Commercial
NSW Commercial site sale, 20a/390 Eastern Valley Way, Chatswood An office/warehouse unit on
Sydney’s north shore has sold to a private investor for $810,000. CBRE’s Peter Mangraviti said the
property had been earmarked for auction, but was sold after only a week.

PRICE: $810,000 BUYER: Private investor SELLER: JM World AREA: 363.5sq m AGENT: Peter
Mangraviti, CBRE
Victoria Shop sale, 1/245 Franklin Street, Melbourne A ground-floor shop in the Infinity8 development
has sold to a private investor for $800,000. The tenant has signed a new five-year lease at a net
annual rental of $44,000 with annual increases in line with CPI.

PRICE: $800,000 BUYER: Private investor SELLER: Private investor AREA: 53sq mAGENT: Guy
Naselli and Raff De Luise, ICR Property
