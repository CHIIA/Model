WESTERN AUSTRALIA Site sale, 63 Adelaide Terrace, East Perth Finbar Group has purchased an
apartment development site in East Perth on behalf of Perth Upper China Hotel for $15 million. The
investment company will own the land and Finbar will develop about 200 apartments in a 32-level
tower with an end value of about $150m. The ground floor of the proposed building will have
commercial tenancies. Finbar managing director Darren Pateman said there was a lack of apartments
in East Perth.
PRICE: $15m BUYER: Finbar Group and Perth Upper China Hotel AREA: 3406sq m

NSW Office lease, 151 Castlereagh Street, Sydney Education company Pearson Australia has leased
space in the Sydney CBD at a gross annual rental of $540,000. The term of the lease is five years.
CBRE’s Tim Molchanoff said the office suite was one of three new commercial floors added during
the building’s renovations.
RENT: $750sq m TENANT: Pearson Australia LESSOR: Valad Property AREA: 720sq m AGENTS:

VICTORIA Retail lease, 488 Centre Road, Bentleigh, Melbourne Fast food chain Domino’s Pizza has
secured space in Melbourne’s southeast at a net annual rental of about $53,000. The term of the lease
is five years. Ray White Commercial agent Ryan Amler said the property had ample parking. “The
property offers a top location with excellent foot traffic,” he said.
