China Life AMP Asset Management Company has launched its inaugural mutual fund. This company is
a joint venture between AMP Capital and China Life Asset Management Company. The China Life AMP
Money Market Fund raised US$1.96 billion from its public offering period, highlighting the opportunity in
the Chinese market. It invests in a range of short-term investments such as short-term bonds and cash
deposits on behalf of retail and institutional customers. AMP Capital chief executive officer Stephen
Dunne said: The launch of China Life AMP Asset Management late last year represented a great step
forward for AMP Capital in Asia, one of our key markets. I m delighted that through the dedication of the
joint venture team, we ve been able to bring the China Life AMP Money Market Fund to Chinese
investors just months after the joint venture was incorporated.

TD

The China Life AMP Money Market Fund analyzes macroeconomic factors, monetary policy and credit
supply on the trends to yield a curve while taking into consideration liquidity and risk profile of different
instruments. It will appeal to investors who are looking for both yield and liquidity. The rapid growth of
wealth management in China presents opportunities for funds management. Total assets under
management in China reached A$800 billion in 2013 and is expected to grow at 15% per annum to
reach A$1.6 trillion by 2017. AMP Capital holds a 15% stake in the joint venture while China Life Asset
Management Company holds the rest. This venture offers retail and institutional investors in China to
lead investment solutions in domestic equities and fixed income and benefits from highly capitalized funds
management company in mainland China.
