NSW Office/retail sale, 10-18 Oxford Square, Darlinghurst A two-storey office and retail building has
sold in inner-east Sydney for $7 million. The property has five separate tenancies, although three are
currently vacant. The site was purchased prior to auction by GNG Investment Holdings, a private
Chinese investor with a range of other assets in Australia.

PRICE: $7m BUYER: GNG Investment Holdings SELLER: HMP Finance AREA: 1308sq m (floor
space), 430sq m (land) YIELD: 8.5 per cent (fully leased) Agent: Michael Khouri and Steam Leung,
Colliers International

Office lease, 609 Harris Street, Ultimo Entrepreneur networking firm The Entourage has signed a
three-year lease for open-plan office space in Ultimo. The 743sq m office has a gross annual rental of
$250,000. CBRE’s Nick Sinclair brokered the lease. “The lessee was attracted to the property’s
proximity to Central Station, and also its open floor space, which suits its conference-style business
needs,” he said.

RENT: $250,000 gross a year TENANT: The Entourage LESSOR: Private investor AREA: 743sq m
AGENT: Nick Sinclair, CBRE

Queensland Retail lease, 60 Queen Street, Brisbane Pure Health Clubs has secured the last vacancy
in the 60 Queen Street retail building in Brisbane’s Queen Street Mall precinct. The health club will
relocate from its current premises at 78 Queen Street once the fitout of the new venue is completed
this month.
