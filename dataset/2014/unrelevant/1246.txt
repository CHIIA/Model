QUEENSLAND Residential development sites, Wensley Road and Odempsey Road, South Ripley
Two parcels of land on the fringes of Brisbane have been sold for about $9 million. Agent Peter
Huang, from Yong Corporate, said he expected the new owners to land bank the sites until the nearby
Ripley Valley development progressed. The 18ha Wensley Road site, sold for $3.55m, is zoned for
medium-density residential. The 168ha Odempsey Road property, sold for $5.2m, is zoned for
low-density residential.
PRICE: $3.55m (Wensley Road), $5.2m (Odempsey Road) BUYER: Private Sydney-based Chinese
investors SELLER: Private investors AREA: 18ha (Wensley Road), 168ha (Odempsey Road) AGENT:
Peter Huang, Yong Corporate
