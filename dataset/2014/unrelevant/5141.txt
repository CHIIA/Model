HIGHLIGHTS Highlights of the AAP Finance Wire for Friday, Nov 14:

LOS ANGELES - Rupert Murdoch has presented eldest son Lachlan to News Corp shareholders as the
man who will lead the media company to "a very prosperous and bright future". (NEWSCORP on file)

LOS ANGELES - Rupert Murdoch has confirmed his part-owned pay TV company Foxtel is looking at
buying a stake in Australia's struggling Ten Network Holdings (FOXTEL TEN on file).
SYDNEY - Mining billionaireGina Rinehart is looking to expand her business empire with a $500
million bid to take advantage of booming demand for baby formula in China (FORMULA WRAP to
come).
PERTH - More than 8000 Glencorecoal workers will be forced to take annual leave next month as the
mining giant shuts down its Australian coal operations due to an oversupply of the
commodity.(GLENCORE UPDATE to come)

SYDNEY - Development giant Lend Lease is lukewarm about the Australian economy despite seeing
local residential construction as a strong point. (LEND LEASE WRAP to come).

stop it, a prominent international economist warns. (ECONOMY JAKOBSEN to come)
