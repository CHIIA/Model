Norton Gold is acquiring Bullabulling Gold in an off-market takeover. With completion of the takeover,
Norton Gold Fields holds 85% of Bullabulling Gold. Board of Bullabulling recommended the 8c per share
offer once acceptances passed 40%. Chinese miner Zijin is holding 82.4% stake in the firm. Apart from
the Bullabulling takeover, and in accordance with the objective of Norton Gold to double production in the
mid term, a term sheet was signed with Excelsior Gold offering a long-term milling allocation at the
Paddington Mill. After acquiring the Paddington Operations in 2007, Norton Gold has poured over one
million ounces.
