LUOYANG, July 24, SinoCast -- China Molybdenum (SHSE: 603993) gained approval to prolong mining
term of Australia-based Northparkes Mines by seven years to 2032.
Located in New South Wales, Northparkes Mines is the fourth-biggest operated copper mine in Australia,
with annual copper concentrate output of six million tons currently. Most of the copper concentrates are
exported to China, Japan and India.
