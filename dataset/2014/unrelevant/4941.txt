NEWS BITES - PEOPLE IN BUSINESS
Australia Minerals and Mining Group recently released their Annual Report. The updated profile of Peter
Bailey, Non-Executive Director is shown below.

Mr Peter BaileyB.Sc(Hons) Elect.Eng MIEE C.Eng Non-Executive Director Mr Peter Bailey was
previously CEO and co-owner at Sherwin Alumina, a smelter and specialty grade alumina plant based
in Texas, USA. Some highlights of this role included heading a joint ownership/private equity
acquisition of Sherwin in 2000; capital raising to purchaseAlcoa's plant; sold 51% of Sherwin to China
MinMetals and ultimately sold the remaining 49% to Glencore in 2007. Mr Bailey was president of Alcoa
Worldwide Chemicals and managed 13 alumina plants around the world. He was also president of Alcoa
Bauxite & Alumina and the chairman of the bauxite joint venture in Guinea, Africa.
