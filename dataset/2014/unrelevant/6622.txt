Lonestar Resources, Ltd. September 2014 Quarterly
Results Conference Call

PR Newswire

FORT WORTH, Texas, Oct. 26, 2014

FORT WORTH, Texas, Oct. 26, 2014 /PRNewswire/ --
Lonestar Resources, Ltd. (ASX: LNR, OTCQX: LNREF)
will announce its September 2014 quarterly results
after-market on October 30, 2014. The report will be
made available via both the ASX announcements
platform and the Company's website at
www.lonestarresources.com.
The results will be released at 3:05 P.M. (U.S. Central
Daylight Time) on Thursday October 30, 2014, which is
at 7:05 A.M. (Australian Eastern Daylight Time) on
Friday October 31, 2014.

Management will host a live conference call on
Thursday October 30, 2014 at 4:00 P.M. (U.S. Central
Daylight Time) which is at 8:00 A.M. (Australian Eastern
Daylight Time) on Friday October 31, 2014 to present
the September 2014 quarterly results and operational
highlights.

Lonestar Resources, Ltd. is a leading independent oil
and gas company involved in exploration, production,
and acquisition of unconventional oil and gas reserves.
Our company's growth is focused on the Crude Oil
Window of the Eagle Ford Shale, where we anticipate
spending 95% of our capital for the next several years.
Lonestar is also evaluating the resource potential of its
50,000 acre block in the Williston Basin.
