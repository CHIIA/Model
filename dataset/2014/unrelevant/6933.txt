Sales

CBD

A private investor bought a 293 sq m office at 100 Collins Street for $1.6 million. CBRE's Tom Tuxworth,
Ed Wright and Chris Pharr brokered the deal worth $5460 per sq m on behalf of the vendor, a women's
medical clinic. The investor plans to refurbish the entire level and lease it out.
A strata retail premises at 353-355 Flinders Lane sold for $1.67million to a private investor on a record
low yield of 4.58 per cent, according to Savills Australia's Nick Peden, Clinton Baxter and Jesse
Radisich. The property, on the corner of Bond Street, has 130 sq m of ground floor retail area leased to
Cafe Del Corso. At 337-339 La Trobe Street, a China-based private buyer has bought the corner
property for $5.6 million. The sale of the 301 sq m, three-level office and retail building set a new land
value record for the CBD on a rate of $18,604 per sq m, Mr Baxter and Mr Peden said.

SOUTH MELBOURNE

An owner-occupier snapped up a 200 sq m office with four secure car spaces at 150 Albert Road, Biggin
& Scott Commercial's Daniel Eramo said. The property sold at a rate of $3500 per sq m.

PORT MELBOURNE
A rare industrial land parcel at 24 Cook Street has sold for a record $960,000 at auction. The 1014 sq m
industrial property with frontage to the West Gate Freeway sold at a rate of $946.75 per sq m. Agents
from CVA Property Consultants and Colliers International brokered the deal which saw interest from
owner-occupiers as well as developers.

NOTTING HILL
An owner-occupier has purchased an 885 sq m industrial and office property at 20 Business Park
Drive for $905,000 in a private sale. Hamish Burgess and Sasan Misaghian from Colliers International
said the freehold property sold at a rate of $1754 per sq m.

Leases

CBD
Frozen Yogurt company, Yoli Yogurt has leased a 52 sq m property at 55 Swanson Street as it
prepares to open its first Victorian store. Savills Australia's Michael Di Carlo brokered the three year
lease, at a rate of $6700 per sq m plus GST.

ABBOTSFORD

MOUNT WAVERLEY

The Central development at 1 Ricketts Road has two new tenants. 21 Lexton has taken a two-year
lease, with the option of an additional three years, for a 122 sq m office with an annual rent of $36,400.
Resource Co has agreed to terms on a 175.5 sq m space, worth $47,385 annually with 3.5 per cent
increases. Knight Frank's Tristan Payne brokered both deals and said Resource Co's leasing terms are
on 3+3+3 year basis.

DANDENONG SOUTH

National book supplier Campion Education has taken up a five year direct lease at 88-92 Waterview
Close. Colliers International's Justin Fried and Paul Jones said the 6061 sq m industrial property
fetched a rate of $73.50 per sq m.

Movers

Savills Australia has a new Divisional Director, Chris Jones from Charter Keck Cramer.

Knight Frank has appointed Joanna Gordon as National Director and Yvette Stamoulis as Director of its
Australian Global Corporate Services division.

CBRE's Chief Operating Officer Daniel Queenan has been promoted to Asia Pacific's Chief Executive
