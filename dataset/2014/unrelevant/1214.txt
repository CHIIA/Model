10 YEARS AGO: The Territory has an obligation to store Australia’s nuclear waste, Federal CLP MP
David Tollner said last night. “I think we have a national responsibility to all Australians to store it here,’’
he said. Mr Tollner said most nuclear waste was generated by health care.

20 YEARS AGO: Nhulunbuy MLA Syd Stirling slammed Federal Administrative Services Minister Frank
Walker for his refusal to help fund the clean-up of a former rocket tracking site, and for the office’s
apparent dismissal of local contractors tendering for extension work.25 YEARS AGO: A group of Hong
Kong businessmen wants to buy an island off Darwin to establish a “new Hong Kong’’. They are
prepared to pay up to $500 million. A Chinese-language newspaper said the group had already met
government officials.
