Exploration and development companyCape Lambert Resources has announced it will return
almost $25 million to shareholders, just a week after a successful settlement with the Australian
Tax Office.
At the beginning of this month the company reached a settlement with the ATO to make a final
payment of $2.4 million, ending a dispute over a remaining liability of $79 million from an
amended assessment in 2012.

Last month it settled a dispute with Metallurgical Corporation of China settlement relating to the
sale of the Cape Lambert magnetite iron ore project.
