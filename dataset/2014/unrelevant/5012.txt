HANGZHOU, August 27, SinoCast -- Beingmate Baby and Child Food Co., Ltd. (SZSE: 002570) revealed
cooperative agreement inked with Fonterra Limited and Fonterra Brands (Hong Kong) Limited under
Fonterra Co-operative Group Ltd, a producer of dairy products headquartered in New Zealand.
Fonterra Brands (Hong Kong) Limited will purchase at most 204.5 million ordinary shares of Beingmate
Baby and Child Food, taking up 20% outstanding shares of the latter.
