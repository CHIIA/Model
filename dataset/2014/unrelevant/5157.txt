Lucy Zhang LiYing, Director

Ms Zhang has been a director of Marvel Earn Ltd since August 2008 and was appointed as director of
Sino-Excel Energy Limited in August 2011. She has strong exposure in investments and advisory
services to Chinese companies seeking overseas listings, capital raisings and potential merger and
acquisition opportunities. She works closely with investment bankers, auditors, lawyers, valuers and
other professionals to enhance the position of clients in preparation for Initial Public Offerings, Reverse
Takeovers or M&A activity.
In addition, Ms Zhang has extensive experience working with the bio-tech, energy, mining, property and
service industries. She has hands-on China management experience and localised business
relationships in the energy and mining sectors.

The Board of OGL Resources Limited (the Company) is pleased to announce the appointment of Ms.
