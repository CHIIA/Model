Gracemere heifers 176c

A REDUCED total of 2950 head of cattle was recorded at what can only be described as an average
quality yarding at Gracemere on September 19. The yarding consisted of 1030 steers, 1450 heifers, 370
cows, 50 cows and calves and 50 bulls. The market held firm to slightly dearer considering the quality of
cattle drawn.

Highlights: Olwin P/L, Winchester Downs, Moranbah, sold 241 Brahman-cross No.2 heifers weighing
383kg to return 164c/$627. Colwil Cattle Co, Leura, Marlborough, sold No.2 Brahman heifers weighing
428kg to make 176c/$755. Kerry Ball, Goovigen, sold two pens of 315kg Red Brangus weaner steers to
top at 180c/$567.
Gary Chalk, Yeppoon, sold No.3 Charbray steers weighing 438kg to a top of 198c/$860. I&S Matsen,
Redbank Station, Dingo, sold a line of quality 242kg Brahman-cross weaner steers to a top of 184c/$430
and 245kg Brahman-cross weaner heifers to make 156c/$371. Mick Colyer and family, Iron Pot, sold
495kg Brahman-cross No.2 steers to top at 195c/$967. Steve Merchin, Alton Downs, sold 603kg
Brangus-cross bullocks to return 198c/$1195. Rob and Una Oats, Comet, sold No.3 Brahman steers
weighing 370kg to make 189c/$700. Wales Grazing Co, Biloela, sold No.3 Brahman steers weighing
346kg to top at 192c/$665. Leanne Creedon and family, Middlemount, sold 398kg No.3 steers Brahman
to 193c/$770.
l The next sale will be held on September 26.

Moreton pasture steers 214c

A YARDING of 905 head of cattle was recorded by agents Boyd O'Brien Bartholomew on Tuesday.
Yearlings and vealers were slightly easier due to one processor not operating. Trade cattle remained
firm as were feeder cattle. All export cattle sold to rates stronger than last week. Yooralla Pastoral Co
sold 377kg yearling heifers to return 184c/$695. Qld Natural Beef sold 271kg yearling steers to make
160c/$435. F&M Drake, Chingee, sold 375kg pasture heifers to return 199c/$747.
G&V Clark, Blackbutt, sold 695kg Euro steers to return 214c/$1488. S&H Edwards sold 525kg Murray
Grey steers to make 206c/$1082. Pharoz Investments sold 475kg Simmental steers to 207c/$983. A
Claydon, Rosewood, sold 290kg grain assist heifers to return 193c/$560. P&M Sheu sold 743kg dairy
culls to 183c/$1361. J Hertrich sold 580kg medium cows to return 193c/$1120. Tudor Downs sold
630kg Euro cows to return 205/$1292. The Hanson Family Trust sold 940kg Santa bulls to make
212c/$1994.
